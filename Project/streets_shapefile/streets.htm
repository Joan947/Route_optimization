Data Quality Information:

Attribute accuracy:

Attribute accuracy report: 

ATTRIBUTES ARE ADDED AT THE TIME THE STREET LINE SEGMENT IS ADDED.

 

Logical consistency report: 

THE CLMDAREA AND CLMCNTY DATASETS ARE MERGED TO CREATE THIS COUNTY WIDE DATASET.

 

Completeness report: 

THIS COVERAGE IS MAINTAINED BY THE CITY OF CHEYENNE GIS DEPARTMENT AND THE LARAMIE COUNTY PLANNING OFFICE. THE STREET 
CENTERLINES ARE EITHER DIGITIZED FROM PLAT MAPS THAT ARE FILED AT THE COUNTY CLERK'S OFFICE OR IMPORTED FROM A CAD FILE. 
THE STREET CENTERLINE SEGMENT FROM POINT AND TO POINT GO IN THE DIRECTION OF HOW THE ADDRESSING RANGE RUNS. EXAMPLE:  
THE STREETS THAT RUN TO THE EAST OF THE ADDRESS GRID LINE WOULD GO FROM THE WEST TO THE EAST. THE STREETS THAT RUN TO 
THE WEST OF THE ADDRESS GRID LINE WOULD GO FROM THE EAST TO THE WEST. THE SAME GOES WITH THE NORTH AND SOUTH ADDRESS GRID LINE.

 

Lineage:

Process step:

Process description: 

THE CITY OF CHEYENNE GIS DEPARTMENT AND LARAMIE COUNTY PLANNING OFFICE WILL RECEIVE A PLAT MAP FILED FROM THE 
COUNTY CLERK'S OFFICE. THIS PLAT MAP WILL EITHER BE DIGITIZED IN TO THE DATASET OR IMPORTED IN FROM A CAD FILE 
OF THE PLAT MAP. THE STREET LINES WILL THEN BE SET TO ITS DIRECTION BASED ON THE ADDRESS HUNDRED BLOCK RANGES. 
NEXT THE OTHER ATTRIBUTES WILL BE ADDED THRU  CUSTIOM  MENUS WRITTEN IN A SCRIPT.
